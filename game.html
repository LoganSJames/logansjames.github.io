Acoustic Preferences

Do you agree with other animals?

Animals communicate with sound, and many prefer certain calls over others. But do humans share those preferences? In this game, you'll hear two sounds from the same species and pick the one you like more. Then we'll reveal which sound the animals themselves preferred.

Based on research with ~4,000 human participants, published in Science. Read the paper

Logan S. James, Sarah C. Woolley, Jon T. Sakata, Courtney B. Hilton, Michael J. Ryan & Samuel A. Mehr

Results

In our study of ~4,000 human participants, people agreed with other animals above chance, suggesting that some aspects of acoustic aesthetics may be shared across species.

some animals have “nearly the same taste for the beautiful as we have.”
